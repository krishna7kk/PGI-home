About Our Organization

Plant Green Inertia Pvt Ltd is a leading training organization dedicated to upskilling students and professionals for the digital age. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between academia and industry requirements, we provide cutting-edge training programs that empower individuals to excel in their careers.

Our unique approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical implementation, ensuring our students are job-ready from day one. With our extensive network of industry partners and academic collaborations, we create unparalleled opportunities for growth and development.

Our Founder

Mr. Laven Lokesh Barani

Mr. Laven Lokesh Barani is a world-class trainer with over seven years of experience since 2017. A fitness enthusiast, he began his entrepreneurial journey in 2015 by manufacturing and selling gym equipment, which he paused in 2017 to focus on training and mentoring entrepreneurs and college students.

Starting his career at the Rural Self Employment Training Institute (RSETI) in 2017, Mr. Lokesh worked closely with small-scale entrepreneurs and business owners. On November 13, 2017, he founded his own training company, offering one-on-one mentorship to students and entrepreneurs.

In 2018, he launched an e-commerce store on Amazon with Dopamin Chocolates, a premium handmade chocolate brand. Alongside, he honed his oratory skills through active participation in Toastmasters Club and Karuna International.
